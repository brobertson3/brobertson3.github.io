Reuse

What Is It?

Repurposing used items as opposed to disposing of them.

Why It's Important

This method has practically the same benefits as reducing. By reusing items, you produce less waste, which save natural resources.

Ways To Do It

  • Invest in reusable items
  • Donate items and buy used
  • Repair before replacing
  • Repurpose things around the house. This link knows what I'm talking about.
