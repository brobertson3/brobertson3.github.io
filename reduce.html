Reduce

What Is It?

Limiting the amount of waste that you produce.

Why It's Important

Reducing waste is one of the most effective ways to help the environment. Making a new product takes a tremendous amount of resources and energy, considering the raw materials required and the transportation of the final product to the consumer.

Things to Consider Before Buying

  • Do you really need it?
  • How often will you be using it?
  • Is there something in your household that functions the same way?

Ways To Do It

  • Borrow items instead of purchasing.
  • Limit your use of plastic bags at stores.
  • Buy items in bulk
  • Avoid buying disposable goods
  • Use paperless billing, remove name from unwanted mailing lists, etc
