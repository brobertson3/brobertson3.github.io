Recycle

What Is It?

A series of steps that takes a used material and processes, remanufactures, and sells it as a new product.

Ways To Do It

  • Buy and use products made from recycled materials. It'll have a recycling symbol on it.
  • Check collection centers and curbside pickup services to see what they accept.

What Can Be Recycled?

  • Paper
  • Plastic
  • Glass
  • Food (Compost)
